Activate display mirroring

To activate display mirroring go to the path /System/Library/CoreServices/SpringBoard.app and open the following file:

iPad: K48AP.plist
iPhone 4: N90AP.plist or N92AP.plist
iPod touch 4G: N81AP.plist

Now add the following code:

    <key>display-mirroring</key>
    <true/>

Do a respring.
