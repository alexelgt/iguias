Respring

A respring restarts the springboard.
When doing a respring the background applications will close.

How might a respring happen?

Manually:
You can only make a respring with jailbreak by manually downloading a package from Cydia.

Apply changes in Cydia:
When you install a package, it is most likely that you will need to do a respring for changes to take effect.

Failure of Mobile Substrate:
When a tweak fails, the device does a respring and puts the device in Safe Mode.
A spinning wheel appears.

Failure of the system:
The device can also do a respring if a serious error occurs.
Apple logo appears.
