Recovery and DFU modes

Recovery mode:

iTunes icon and an image of a cable appear on the screen of the device.

This mode accesses the iBoot (bootloader) of the device.
It only allows restoring to the same version or higher.

DFU mode (Device Firmware Update):

The screen of the device appears completely black.

This mode does not access the iBoot (bootloader) of the device.
Allows restoring to any version if possible.
