Webclip

A webclip is a shortcut that allows you to go to a website that is on the springboard.

Webclips are saved in the path /var/mobile/Library/WebClips.

To save a web page on the springboard, go to Safari and press this button:

Now press "Add to Home Screen".

After having set the name you want, press the button called "Add" and an icon will appear on the springboard.
