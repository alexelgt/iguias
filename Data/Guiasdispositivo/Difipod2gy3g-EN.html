iPod touch 2G and 3G

iPod touch 3G:

    • Headphones: Apple earphones with remote and mic.
    • Display: fingerprint-resistant oleophobic coating.
    • Features: OpenGL ES 2.0, voice control, better battery, accessibility, home screen background (iOS 4) and multitasking (iOS 4).

iPod touch 2G:

    • Headphones: Apple earphones without remote and mic.
    • Features: OpenGL ES 1.2.

Source: www.apple.com
