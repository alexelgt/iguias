iPad 2, 3 and 4

iPad 4:

    • Depth: 0.94 cm.
    • Weight: 652-662 gramos.
    • Camera: 5 megapixel with autofocus, tap to focus, face detection in still images, HD video recording (1080p), video stabilization. The size of the camera is the same as the iPad 3.
    • Display: 2048x1536 pixel resolution at 264 pixels per inch.
    • Cellular and wireless: Bluetooth 4.0.

      Modelo A1459: GSM/EDGE (850, 900, 1800, 1900 MHz), UMTS/HSPA+/DC-HSDPA (850, 900, 1900, 2100 MHz), LTE (bands 4, 17)

      Modelo A1460: CDMA EV-DO Rev. A and Rev. B (800, 1900, 2100 MHz), GSM/EDGE (850, 900, 1800, 1900 MHz), UMTS/HSPA+/DC-HSDPA (850, 900, 1900, 2100 MHz), LTE (bands 1, 3, 5, 13, 25)

    • Features: Siri (iOS 6), lightning connector.

iPad 3:

    • Depth: 0.94 cm.
    • Weight: 652-662 gramos.
    • Camera: 5 megapixel with autofocus, tap to focus, face detection in still images, HD video recording (1080p). The size of the camera is bigger.
    • Display: 2048x1536 pixel resolution at 264 pixels per inch.
    • Cellular and wireless: Bluetooth 4.0, 4G LTE (700, 2100 MHz).
    • Features: Siri (iOS 6).

iPad 2:

    • Depth: 0.88 cm.
    • Weight: 601-613 gramos.
    • Camera: HD video recording (720p).
    • Display: 1024x768 pixel resolution at 132 pixels per inch.
    • Cellular and wireless: Bluetooth 2.1 + EDR.

Source: www.apple.com
