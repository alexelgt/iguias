iPhone 3G and 3GS

iPhone 3GS

    • Weight: 135 grams.
    • Camera: 3 megapixel, tap to focus and video recording.
    • Display: fingerprint-resistant oleophobic coating.
    • Cellular and wireless: Bluetooth 2.1 + EDR.
    • Features: OpenGL ES 2.0, voice control, better battery, accessibility, compass, home screen background (iOS 4) and multitasking (iOS 4).

iPhone 3G

    • Weight: 133 grams.
    • Camera: 2 megapixel.
    • Cellular and wireless: Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR.
    • Features: OpenGL ES 1.2.

Source: www.apple.com
